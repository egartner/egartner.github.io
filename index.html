About me

Thank you for visiting! My name is Etienne and I am a Computer Scientist based in Hong Kong. I've always been fascinated by Artificial Intelligence and Natural Sciences. I've now graduated from my computer science engineering degree in France and I want to use my knowledge to solve problems in biology and physics. Having lived in a close relationship with illness, there is nothing more important than health to me. I believe technology is a critical enhancer for this sector and my goal is to contribute to interdisciplinary initiatives aiming at improving any aspect of health. I see this line of work as both fun and meaningful.
